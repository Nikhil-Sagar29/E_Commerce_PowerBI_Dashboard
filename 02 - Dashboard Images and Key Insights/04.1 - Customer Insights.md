# Customer Analysis
### Key Insights
1. Highest Spending Customers: Top spenders purchase fewer but higher-value products.
2. Most Orders Customers: Customers with the most orders do not generate the highest revenue.
3. Customer Segment: High-value customers mainly belong to the “High Value” segment.
4. Product Categories: Top customers usually purchase from 2–3 categories.
5. Payment Preference: Most high-value customers use credit cards.
