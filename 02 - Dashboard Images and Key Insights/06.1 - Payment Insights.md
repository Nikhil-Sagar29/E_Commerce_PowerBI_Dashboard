# Payment Analysis
### Key Insights

1. Total Payment Value: Total payments amount to 16M.
2. Total Transactions: Nearly 99K transactions were completed.
3. Average Installment: Customers pay in 3 installments on average.
4. Top Payment Method: Credit card is the most used and highest revenue-generating payment type.
5. Revenue by Installments: Most revenue comes from 1-installment payments.
6. Distribution: Voucher and debit card have very low usage.
