# Delivery Analysis
### Key Insights
1. Delivered Orders: 96,478 orders were delivered successfully.
2. Cancelled Orders: Only 625 orders were cancelled.
3. Delivery Accuracy: 93% of orders were delivered on time or earlier.
4. Delayed Deliveries: Only 7% of orders were delayed.
5. Average Delay: Late deliveries are delayed by 11 days on average.
6. Maximum Delay: The worst delivery delay reached 188 days.
7. Delivery Category: Most orders were delivered earlier than expected.
8. Root Cause: cama_mesa_banho and beleza_saude categories contribute most to delays.
