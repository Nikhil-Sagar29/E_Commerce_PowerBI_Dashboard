##  🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

📌 Project Overview
-----
This project presents an end-to-end E-Commerce Data Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI. It provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, payment trends, and customer reviews.

The goal of this project is to transform raw data into actionable business insights for better decision-making.

❓ Business Problems 
----
1. Low Repeat Purchase Rate
  - The company has a large customer base, but most customers place only one order.

2. Revenue Dependency on Few Categories
 - A small number of product categories contribute most of the revenue, creating business risk.

3. Delivery Delays Reducing Customer Satisfaction
 - Even though most deliveries are successful, delayed deliveries significantly reduce review scores.

4. Lack of Understanding of High-Value Customers
 - The business does not clearly know which customers generate the most revenue and what they purchase.

5. Payment Method Optimization
 - The company wants to know which payment methods customers prefer and how payment behavior affects revenue.

6. Negative Reviews Without Clear Root Cause
 - The business receives negative reviews but lacks visibility into what causes them.

----
💡 How This Dashboard Solves These Problems
----
| Business Problem |	Dashboard Solution |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Low repeat purchase rate |	Customer Analysis page identifies that most customers purchase only once and highlights high-value customer groups                                 for retention campaigns.
| Revenue concentrated in a few categories    |	Sales Analysis page shows top-performing categories and helps identify underperforming categories                                                  that need improvement.
| Delivery delays hurting customer satisfaction |	Delivery Analysis page tracks delayed orders, average delay, and identifies categories/cities                                                      causing the delays.
| Difficulty identifying valuable customers    	| Customer Analysis page segments customers into High, Medium, and Low Value groups based on                                                         spending and order behavior.
| No visibility into payment preferences |	Payment Analysis page identifies the most used payment methods, revenue contribution, and installment                                               behavior.
| Negative reviews without explanation	| Review Analysis page connects review scores with delivery delays and identifies which categories receive                                           poor ratings.


📊 Dashboard Pages
--

1️⃣ E-Commerce Overview
--

1. Total Sales: 16M+
2. Total Orders: 99K+
3. Total Customers: 96K+
4. Top-performing city and product categories
5. Most used payment method is Credit Card (75% of all transactions)

- Provides a high-level snapshot of overall business performance.

Key Business Insight
-------
Most customers place only one order, which means the company should focus on customer retention strategies.

2️⃣ Sales Analysis
---

1. Yearly and Monthly Sales Trends
2. Revenue per Customer
3. Average Order Value
4. Category-wise Sales vs Quantity

- Helps identify growth trends, seasonality, and top-performing categories.

Business Problem Solved
-------
The dashboard identifies which product categories drive the highest revenue and which categories have low sales despite high order volume.

Recommended Action
-------
1. Increase inventory and marketing for high-performing categories.
2. Improve or bundle low-performing categories.

3️⃣ Customer Analysis
--
1. Top customers by spending
2. Customers with highest number of orders
3. Customer segmentation (High, Medium, Low value)
4. Category-wise spending behavior

- Highlights customer purchasing patterns and segmentation.

Business Problem Solved
--
The business can now distinguish between:

1. Customers who buy frequently
2. Customers who spend the most
3. Recommended Action
4. Reward high-spending customers with loyalty offers.
5. Encourage repeat purchases from one-time buyers.
   
4️⃣ Delivery Analysis
--

1. Delivery Accuracy: 93%
2. Delayed Deliveries: 7%
3. Average Delay: 11 days
4. Maximum Delay: 188 days
5. Root cause analysis using Decomposition Tree

- Focuses on logistics performance and delay analysis.

Business Problem Solved
--

The dashboard identifies which product categories and cities contribute most to delivery delays.

Recommended Action
--
1. Improve logistics for categories with major delays.
2. Review shipping partners in frequently delayed cities.
   
5️⃣ Payment Analysis
--
1. Total Transactions: 99K+
2. Total Payment Value: 16M+
3. Most used payment method: Credit Card
4. Installment-based payment analysis

- Explains how customers prefer to pay and revenue distribution.

Business Problem Solved
---
The business understands which payment methods customers prefer and which methods generate the highest revenue.

Recommended Action
--
1. Promote the most preferred payment method for smoother checkout.
2. Offer installment options for expensive categories.
   
6️⃣ Review Analysis
--

1. Total Reviews: 98K+
2. Average Rating: 4.09 ⭐
3. Positive Reviews: 77%
4. Negative Reviews: 15%
5. Review trends vs delivery performance

- Connects customer satisfaction with delivery and product experience.

Business Problem Solved
--
The dashboard proves that delayed deliveries directly reduce customer ratings.

Recommended Action
--

1. Reduce delivery delays to improve review scores.
2. Focus on categories with high negative review percentages.
   
🧠 Key Insights
---
1. Most customers place only one order, indicating low repeat purchase rate.
2. Credit cards dominate payment methods (>75% usage).
3. A few product categories contribute to the majority of revenue.
4. 93% delivery success rate, but delays significantly impact reviews.
5. Early deliveries lead to higher customer satisfaction scores.
6. High-spending customers are different from high-frequency buyers.
